Our Project Highlights

Impact

Our interactive habitat design simulator empowers users to engage with the complexities of building space habitats, addressing challenge of building effective living space in extraterrestrial environments. By simulating the arrangement process of elements, users can visualize and optimize habitat layouts, considering factors like privacy requirements and noise. This hand-on experience not only enhances understanding of space habitat design, but also fosters critical thinking and problem solving skills, preparing individuals for future engineering challenges.

Innovation

The game innovates by transforming complex space habitats into an interactive, 3D experience. It allows users to experiment with habitat layouts and life support systems, arrange them based on strict instructions, and receive AI feedback. By combining realistic physics, environmental constraints, and intuitive design tools, the platform bridges the gap between professional engineering practices and public understanding, making space exploration education engaging, hands-on, and scalable.

Alignment with challage

The game aligns with the “Your Home in Space: The Habitat Layout Creator” well enabling players to design habitat’s shape and volume, explore multiple layouts, and optimize for habitability and efficiency. It turns NASA’s objectives into an educational, 3D experience allowing users to engage with realistic constraints, experiment with solutions, and have a deeper understanding of sustainable space living
